Detection Signals

Enterprise-Grade Fraud Detection

Every transaction is scored across multiple dimensions — from velocity to geography — for layered protection.

🤖

Random Forest ML Engine

200-estimator classifier trained on behavioral patterns — amount, velocity, time-of-day — with balanced class weights.

⚡

Real-Time Rule Engine

7 deterministic rules covering extreme amounts, high velocity, odd-hour windows, and unknown entity detection.

📊

Hybrid Risk Scoring

Combines 60% heuristic rules + 40% ML probability into a single 0–100 risk score with LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / CRITICAL labels.

🌍

Geographic Intelligence

Flags international transactions paired with elevated amounts as high-risk indicators automatically.

🔍

Explainable Decisions

Every prediction surfaces the exact rules triggered — no black-box outputs, full audit trail.

🛡️

Academic Safe Design

Built on synthetic, privacy-preserving datasets — ideal for educational demos without exposing real financial data.

Process

How It Works

Three stages from raw transaction to actionable risk label.

01

Ingest Transaction

Amount, hour, velocity, location and entity identity are extracted from the incoming payment event.

02

Dual Analysis

The rule engine fires 7 deterministic checks while the Random Forest model assigns a fraud probability in parallel.

03

Risk Score & Label

Scores are blended into a 0–100 composite, mapped to NORMAL / SUSPICIOUS / FRAUD, and surfaced on the dashboard.
